Hi, I’m Laura! I'm based in Alabama with over six years of experience specializing in fraud prevention, data analysis, and cybersecurity. My career began with Shipt, where I grew in various roles, each reinforcing my analytical and problem-solving skills. In my most recent role as a Security Engineer, I focused on implementing proactive fraud detection systems with Golang, automating workflows with Python, and collaborating with data science and engineering teams to tackle security challenges.

I’m well-versed in analysis tools like SQL, Python, and Tableau, which I use to extract and visualize data insights that help my team make informed, data-driven decisions. My passion for data analysis is complemented by my certifications in topics ranging from data science to full stack web development.

Outside of tech, I enjoy working on projects around my house, tending to my garden, and taking care of my three dogs. I love to explore new hiking trails and I'm always on the lookout for new fun ways to enjoy the outdoors.
